FinGuard AI Project

FinGuard AI is a state-of-the-art anomaly and fraud detection system designed to monitor, track, and protect financial transactions in real-time. By leveraging a robust ensemble of cutting-edge Multi-Model Machine Learning algorithms, it delivers high-accuracy insights and explainable AI capabilities natively.

Our 4 Core AI Models

  • LSTM + RF: An optimized hybrid model combining Long Short-Term Memory networks for processing sequential transaction telemetry, alongside Random Forest to form an unyielding decision baseline.
  • CNN + BiLSTM: Identifies spatial and relational feature patterns locally via Convolutions, and outsmarts complex time-series behavior using Bidirectional LSTM.
  • TCN + BiLSTM + Multihead Attention: A PyTorch-based powerhouse combining Temporal Convolutions, BiLSTMs, and Multihead Attention mechanisms for top-tier anomaly isolation.
  • TCN: Applies highly tailored temporal filtering capabilities with dilation paths to isolate sudden volumetric spikes and organized fraud rings.
